Questions? Answers.

Do I really not need an account?
Nope! Just open the app and start organizing. No email, no password, nothing. We designed it to be as frictionless as possible.
Where is my data stored?
Everything is stored in your browser's local storage. It never leaves your device. This means perfect privacy but also means you can't access it from a different browser/device yet.
Will it always be free?
Yes. Since we don't have server costs (everything runs in your browser), we can keep it free forever. We might add optional paid features in the future, but the core will always be free.
Can I use it for team collaboration?
Not yet! Right now Vibe is designed for solo use. We're exploring privacy-respecting collaboration features for the future.
What browsers does it work on?
Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge - all modern browsers work great. Mobile browsers too!
Is my data really private?
Absolutely. We don't have servers to store your data on. It's architecturally impossible for us to see your tasks. You don't even need to trust us - the code proves it.
